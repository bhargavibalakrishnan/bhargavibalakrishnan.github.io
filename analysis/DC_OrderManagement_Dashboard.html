Unlocking Order Insights: Interactive Dashboard for Smarter Operations

Track Costs, Analyze Trends, and Optimize Your Supply Chain with this Tool

Problem Statement

This Looker dashboard aims to analyze the performance of purchase orders from 2019 to 2023 and provide insights into order costs across different stages (completed, shipped, and processing) and locations.

Analysis

The interactive dashboard allows users to:

1. Visualize order cost trends: Utilize an area chart to visualize order costs across the selected date range, helping to identify periods of high or low spending.

2. Analyze geographic distribution: Explore a geo map displaying the cost of shipped orders arriving at US Distribution Centers. This enables users to identify regional trends and potential cost variations.

3. Monitor order costs: Utilize a scorecard to quickly monitor the total cost of completed, shipped, and processing orders.

Looker Dashboard

Conclusion

These findings can be used to:

1. Identify areas for process optimization to reduce processing times and associated costs

2. Optimize resource allocation: Allocate resources strategically based on regional cost variations and order completion rates

3. Negotiate better pricing: Utilize cost data to leverage negotiation power with suppliers
